Publication: Formation of local quiet zones using the length-limited parametric array loudspeaker

Sep 13, 2019, Proceedings of the 23rd International Congress on Acoustics : integrating 4th EAA Euroregio 2019

This paper presents a preliminary numerical study on the formation of local quiet zones using the lengthlimited parametric array loudspeaker (PAL). The PAL is a directional sound device using the nonlinear acoustic effect to generate an audio beam in air. The length-limited PAL aims to control the length of the audio beam by using two different ultrasonic carriers. The sound propagation curve and radiation pattern of the length-limited PAL are theoretically distinct from those of the conventional loudspeaker. Therefore, the local quiet zones formed by the length-limited PAL is of sufficient interest before any practical deployment of the length-limited PAL in active noise control (ANC). For comparison, the local quiet zones formed by the conventional loudspeaker and the normal PAL are also obtained and examined in the numerical simulation.

Other authors: Wang Yue, Shi Chuang, Lv Youxin

Patent: Controllable maximum weight signal real-time gating multi-channel active noise control system

Sep 22, 2019, Patent issuer and number: cn 201910896276.5

The invention discloses a controllable maximum weight signal real-time gating multi-channel active noise control system and belongs to the technical field of active noise control. In order to solve the technical problem that along with the increase of the number of microphones in the multi-channel active noise control system, the number of ADC circuits is linearly increased and the arithmetic operand is obviously increased, the invention proposes a new multi-channel active noise control system, wherein a circuit system for monitoring the magnitude of multiple paths of sound wave voltage signals in real time and keeping the maximum path of voltage output is provided, and meanwhile, the circuit system also has the function of being capable of manually controlling gating any one path of signals. According to the multi-channel active noise control system in the invention, comparison of the magnitude of multiple sound wave signals in the multi-channel active noise control system and a gating process are completed through a hardware circuit, so that the core controller resources are not occupied; and meanwhile, the control system only partially updates a control filter corresponding to the maximum error signal, so that the operation cost in the multi-channel active noise control system is effectively reduced.

Other authors: Shi Chuang, Wang Yue, Jiang Nan, Xie Rong, Li Huiyong
